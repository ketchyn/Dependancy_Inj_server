Analysis of investment projects is an integral part of managing investments. It gives information base (in draft form as specially decorated investment plan or the results of the examination of the project) for a decision on the inclusion of the project in the portfolio, beginning his investment, continuous monitoring of implementation.

Analysis of investment projects – a set of methodological and practical design techniques, study and assess the feasibility of the project.

Only a fraction of the investment is unsuccessful, that is does not give the expected result for reasons beyond the control of the investor.

Most of the projects were unprofitable. could not be allowed to implement quality provided preliminary analysis taking into account the external environment and the quality of projects.

Investment projects are analyzed on the basis of a comprehensive study of aspects of the future of investing.
